Democratic Funding

We've used Democratic Funding to raise and distribute over $11,000,000 to open source software and non-profit communities. Additionally, it raised over $40,000 to local businesses disrupted by COVID in a pilot program run in Boulder, Colorado.

We’re a team obsessed with finding inventive, creative ways to fund Public Goods, and we're currently exploring how to bring Democratic Funding to your state & local government.
